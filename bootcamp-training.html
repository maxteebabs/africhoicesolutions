Bootcamp Training

At Africhoice Solutions, we believe that world-class empowerment is key to successful development. Our focus is to discover young talents and equip them with skills to become successful developers in the industry.

Our bootcamp training is a 6-weekends full-time program designed to equip you with skills to become an entry-level developer. Its an intensive training that exposes you to hands-on pratice exercises. Therefore, be prepared for alot of coding and hardwork.

The aim of this bootcamp training is to enable you achieve so much in less time. It will also get you to a point where you can boast of your own "App Portfolio". We will equip you with the knowledge/skill and provide you with a conducive learning environment with internet access you need to become a full-fledge web/mobile developer.

Our approach has proven to be very effective with numerous success stories.Register now and be part of this success story.

 

Collaborative Environment

With expert facilitators, research, and classmates, there’s always someone to get you unstuck. Our program simulates a real work environment where people problem-solve as a team.
